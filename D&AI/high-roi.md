For **Sketch Intelligence**, I would not optimize for "chapters" first. I would optimize for **search demand + conceptual dependency + future relevance**.

Think of your sketches as a **visual encyclopedia of intelligence systems**.

A good sketch topic should satisfy at least one of these:

* Frequently searched
* Frequently misunderstood
* Frequently used in AI discussions
* Acts as a bridge to many other concepts
* Has strong visual storytelling potential

# Nutrition Operating System (Fat Loss + BP Reduction + Muscle Preservation)

## Primary Goals

* Reduce BP naturally and improve vascular health
* Lose ~6–7 kg over 10–12 weeks
* Reduce visceral fat and waist size
* Preserve/build muscle with moderate exercise
* Improve sleep, recovery, and energy
* Create a low-friction, repeatable lifestyle

---

# Core Nutrition Principles

* Protein-first eating at every meal
* Predominantly whole-food plant-forward diet
* Moderate calorie deficit (~1800–2000 kcal/day)
* High fiber + high satiety foods
* Early light dinner
* Minimal processed foods
* Repetition over variety

# Non-Negotiable Rules

1. Breakfast stays protein-heavy.
2. Lunch is the largest meal.
3. Dinner stays lighter than lunch.
4. Vegetables at lunch and dinner are mandatory.
5. No sugary drinks or liquid calories.
6. Avoid processed foods and fried snacks.
7. Repeat meals often — consistency > novelty.
8. Aim for ~80% adherence, not perfection.
